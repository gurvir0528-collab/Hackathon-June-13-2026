How to Play
Learn the basics of Street Sweeper
In Street Sweeper, your goal is to gain as many points as possible!
How do you do this? You need to code your robot to pick up garbage! You have to assemble a list of simple commands such as moveForward() to drive your robot to garbage, and pick it up.
Collecting trash gains you XP! Whoever has the most XP will be displayed on the leaderboard.
Thats it! Drive your way to victory while learning simple code, and cleaning up the Earth.
Available Commands:
moveForward(steps);
turn(degrees);
